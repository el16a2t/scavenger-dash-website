How It Works

Three simple steps to the ultimate London adventure

Assemble Your Teams

Gather your friends, family or colleagues at a start point of your choosing. Join your exclusive game session by sending your game code to the Scavenger Dash number on Whatsapp and set up your game.

Dash Across London

Split into your teams and race across the city collecting bizarre items from your list. No one finds out what items you need to pick up en route to the finish until you start the race!

Strike the right balance between charming and bartering with strangers to keep costs low and prioritising speed by paying for items. Alternatively, in any dash, if you choose Easy Mode, you only need to get a picture with each of your items en route.

Meet at the Finish

Race with your collected items to a pub carefully selected by Scavenger Dash based on your preferred region of London. The first there with as many of the items wins the game! Compare your hauls and celebrate the winners.
